SMART
OBJECTS

Smart Objects in Photopea are special layers that preserve the original content of an image or graphic, allowing for non-destructive editing. When you convert a layer (or multiple layers) into a Smart Object, you can resize, transform, warp, or apply filters without permanently altering the original data. This means you can always go back and make changes to the source content without losing quality or detail.

CANVA
FRAMES

Creating custom frames for Canva using Photopea allows you to design unique, reusable shapes that can act as clipping masks for photos and graphics within Canva. These frames are essentially transparent PNGs or SVGs with distinct cut-out areas, letting users drag and drop images that automatically snap into place.
